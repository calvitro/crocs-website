Kyle Stopak is a Graduate Research Assistant at the National Counterterrorism Innovation, Technology, and Education (NCITE) Center of Excellence and an incoming M.S. Student in the School of Criminology and Criminal Justice at the University of Nebraska at Omaha. A member of the CROCS Lab, his research interests include radicalization pathways, counterterrorism, behavioral threat assessment, and emerging threats to homeland security.
